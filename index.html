About the Department

History & Mission

Established with the college in 2007, the Computer Science Department aims to provide accessible and quality education. Our mission is to equip students from all backgrounds with the skills needed for a successful career in the tech industry.

Courses Offered

  • BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)
  • B.Sc. (Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science)
  • Both programs are affiliated with Bengaluru City University and follow a curriculum designed for holistic development.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computer education, nurturing talent and fostering innovation to create socially responsible professionals who can contribute to the technological advancement of the nation.
